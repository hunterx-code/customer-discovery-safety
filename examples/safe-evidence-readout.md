# Safe Evidence Readout

## What We Know

- Three operators mentioned month-end dashboard cleanup as slow.
- One buyer asked about SOC 2 before describing a current workflow problem.
- One community comment expressed interest without role, budget, or urgency.
- One partner directory pointed to a procurement inbox.

## What We Do Not Know

- Whether the target user has weekly pain or only occasional reporting pressure.
- Whether a buyer owns budget for this problem.
- Whether the procurement inbox reaches the correct role.
- Whether a pilot or paid test is realistic.

## Evidence Classification

- Demand evidence: possible workflow pain, not yet strong enough.
- Route evidence: one procurement route to test.
- Channel failure: none observed.
- Refusal / non-fit: none observed.
- Weak signal: compliance question and community interest without concrete intent.

## Decision

Do not launch broad outreach. Continue only with one approved, direct interview route that asks about current behavior before showing the concept.
