# Short Post Drafts

## Short

Built a Codex skill for customer discovery work where agents can move too fast.

It stops weak notes from turning into unauthorized outreach or fake validation.

Repo: https://github.com/hunterx-code/customer-discovery-safety

## Technical

Customer Discovery Safety is a Codex skill for agent-led customer discovery.

It gives the agent a circuit breaker: classify weak vs strong evidence, draft body-visible approval packets, and scan public files before private operational details leak.

Repo: https://github.com/hunterx-code/customer-discovery-safety

## Blunt

Most customer-discovery automation fails by moving too fast: it sends too early, overreads weak signals, or leaks private notes.

This skill slows down the risky parts while keeping local drafting fast.

Repo: https://github.com/hunterx-code/customer-discovery-safety
